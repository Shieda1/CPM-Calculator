What is the cost per thousand calculator?
The cost per thousand calculator is a tool that computes the cost of an online marketing campaign.
How to calculate cost per thousand?
To calculate cost per thousand you should use the following formula:
cost per thousand = (total cost / ad impressions) * 1000
Why use cost per thousand calculator?
The cost per thousand calculator is used in order to measure the cost of an online marketing campaign.
